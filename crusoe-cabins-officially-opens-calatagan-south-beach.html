Crusoe Cabins officially opens at Calatagan South Beach

Pioneering real estate developer Landco opens the newest destination for beach getaways, the Crusoe Cabins located at picturesque Calatagan South Beach (CaSoBe).

Just across Landco’s Playa Calatagan Village, Crusoe is Landco’s newest beach resort venture which features 13 stand-alone beachside cabin accommodations.

Crusoe has two-unit configurations: “Group” cabins that have two bunk beds good for six people and “Family” units that have a bunk bed and a queen-sized bed that can accommodate five people.

Every cabin is complete with a bedroom, a living room, kitchenette and a porch terrace.

Guests can also enjoy:

  • The Boardwalk, a beach promenade that features cabanas for day visits, with support service amenities that include outdoor showers, for rent water sports equipment and the following restaurants:
  • The Sands, a Filipino-themed seaside restaurant featuring freshly-prepared cuisines that can be enjoyed along the beach of Calatagan;
  • The Swing Bar, an iconic statement piece surrounded by swings as its seats that serves cocktails and light snacks; and
  • The ConBar, a beer shack that serves local and international labels
  • Aquaria Water Park, a water park that features 5 cove pools, a kiddie pool and a giant 3-storey high pool slide.
Visit Crusoe Cabins and have a memorable vacation today. For inquiries and reservations, you can contact Anya Hospitality at (02) 553-8888 local 23 & 12 from Monday to Friday at 8am to 6pm, or email at reservations.crusoe@fuegohotels.com.
