A Leisure-filled Life in Full Bloom!

Living at the Ponderosa Leisure Farms affords you a lifestyle of ease and leisure.

Situated on a 71-hectare floral paradise, Ponderosa Leisure Farms is the first residential nature community located in Silang, Cavite. It seamlessly marries the unspoiled splendor of beautiful gardens, rustic Spanish-Mexican architecture and world-class amenities. Ponderosa Farms is easily accessible via Tagaytay proper or from the Sta. Rosa Exit of the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX). Wake up to a world in full bloom, with the cool Tagaytay weather and breathtaking gardens which create the perfect nature-inspired backdrop for you and your family.

Enjoy World-class Resort-Type Amenities

Living at the Ponderosa Leisure Farms affords you a lifestyle of ease and leisure. 24-hour security, maintenance and care services for hassle-free gardening, and bougainvillea and pine tree-landscaped sidewalks are only a few of the many reasons why you should call this a luxury development home. Enjoy a host of first-class amenities, including the lovely Villa Ponderosa Clubhouse, the stately Plaza Maravilla, the serene Tiendadel Flores and three themed gardens – El Refugio, El Sanctuario and Campos Verdes. Escape the sizzling summer heat by taking a dip in the adult or children’s pools. Relish the company of family and friends at our child-friendly playground or cozy tea house, an ideal venue for small gatherings. Thoughtful touches like these make Ponderosa Leisure farms a place to detox and relax.
