Own your own Hacienda!

Located along the highly accessible South Luzon Expressway in Muntinlupa, TRIbeca Private Residences is conveniently located near the Makati and Alabang CBDs, private schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and airports. TRIbeca’s residential resort amenities redefine the dynamics of urban resort living. This top-notch property boasts of a 1800-square meter Aqua Park, three-hectare Central Park with open and active spaces, and clubhouse. TRIbeca Private Residences has set a new standard for condominium living in the South, providing a leisure and resort setting for today’s urbanites. Living in TRIbeca means being able to experience the luxury resort and leisure lifestyle that Landco is known for.
