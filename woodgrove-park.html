Enjoy the best of San Fernando

Situated in San Fernando, Pampanga, Woodgrove Park is Pampanga’s newest oasis. This exclusive enclave projects residential luxury in a hundred different ways. Designed to reflect the timeless elegance and architectural glory of the American South, Woodgrove Park’s elegantly crafted homes exude a distinct Southern American charm. Adorned by porches and wood panels, Woodgrove Park features wide tree-lined streets and expansive gardens.

A variety of amenities that matches a cozy and tranquil Southern life are savored at WoodGrove Park.

To cozy and tranquil vibe of Woodgrove Park are complemented by amenities that allow the community to enjoy what the property has to offer, like the vast expanse of space of the development through its well-manicured gardens and connecting jogging trails.

Take a dip into the cool waters of Woodgrove’s free-form adult and kiddie pool and savor the laughs and fun times at Woodgrove’s Clubhouse.

Woodgrove Park’s proximity to North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and the city’s main thoroughfare, the Olongapo-Gapan Road makes it highly-accessible to the conveniences and attractions San Fernando has to offer.

Enjoy the best of San Fernando. Live in Woodgrove Park.
