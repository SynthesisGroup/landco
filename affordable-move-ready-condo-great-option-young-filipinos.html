This Affordable, Move-in-Ready Condo Is a Great Option for Young Filipinos

Rent-to-own condos offer the best option for young professionals aspiring to become homeowners

Experience what it’s like to live in a condo and have everything you need just right outside your doorstep and more

In your own network, there is a high probability of you knowing one or two who live in a condo. It has become a trend that dictates the lifestyle of many young Filipinos nowadays, especially those in Metro Manila, because of the many advantages, features, and services a condo offers and provides.

They have probably talked about how living in a condo lessens their travel time to and from work because of its accessible location. This is a great advantage because the shorter the time you spend on traveling or commuting means a longer time in your hands for rest or running errands.

The great thing about Metro Manila’s recent condo boom is that it provides a wide range of property choices for everyone. Aside from topnotch locations, many condominiums today, in fact, offer resort-like amenities that residents enjoy immensely, making it hard for them to even leave. Not just content with offering normal lap pools, many condos now offer Olympic-sized swimming pools, basketball and tennis courts, and even football fields. Retail rows within or around a condo building are now included for the convenience of the residents, making everything that they need just within reach.

Despite the amazing features and amenities condos offer, it might be surprising to know that one can live in such a place without having to shell out a huge amount of money. In fact, one can be the proud owner of a condo unit starting with just Php12,000 per month.

Of course, Php12,000 may still be a huge amount for some. But taking a step back to look at the list of monthly expenses might reveal that a few unnecessary expenses will amount to (or exceed) Php12,000. Here are the usual things that young, single Filipinos spend on every month.

Eliminating (or at least minimizing) the expenses listed above, there is a huge possibility of finally being able to afford your very own condo unit. It just takes willpower, self-discipline, and resourcefulness to gain and enjoy the perks of being a unit owner, such as having exclusive access and membership to beach resorts, clubs, and entertainment establishments across the nation, just like at Tribeca Private Residences, a condominium community conveniently located along East Service Road in Sucat, Muntinlupa.

Tribeca Private Residences, a 9.7-hectare development by Landco Pacific Corporation., is a resort-inspired residence with towers that rise up to 17 stories. Homebuyers or real estate investors may choose among spacious studio, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom units. In fact, their smallest units in Towers 1 to 4 measure 25.5 sqm, while those in Tower 5 are even larger at 33 sqm, which are perfect for young urban professionals.

The main features of Tribeca include the luxurious 1,800-sqm swimming pool complex dubbed Aqua Park, which has plenty of water amenities such as pool beach, water slide, Jacuzzi, pool bar, massage cabanas and sunken deck; and the three-hectare Central Park, which has tree-lined walkways, a social pavilion, barbecue pits, tree court, life-size dollhouses, 7-a-side football field, and fitness circuit.

Acquiring a unit in Tribeca also entitles you to become a member of the Landco Privilege Club (once you are already fully paid with your unit), an exclusive club for Landco residents. Being a member gives you exclusive access to Landco’s leisure resort communities, such as Club Punta Fuego, Playa Calatagan, and Playa Laiya in Batangas, and Playa Azalea in Samal Island, Davao del Norte. You can avail of their discounted or even free services and activities when you present your Landco Privilege Club membership card at your chosen leisure resort community. You may also enjoy discounts from partner clubs and establishments of Landco across the country.

For as low as Php12,000 a month, you could be one of those who are fortunate enough to experience the great life a condominium can give.
