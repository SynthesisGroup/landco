LANDCO Pacific Corporation forges ahead to pursue its vision of attaining the highly-coveted Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) certification of its Leisure Tourism Estates Calatagan South Beach (CaSoBe) and Club Laiya in Batangas through LEED registration and sustainability initiatives of the two properties.

Landco President and CEO Erickson Manzano said "The LEED registration of our LTE in Batangas is part of our long-term and holistic commitment to economic, social and environmental sustainability program. Landco initiated this program, not to simply achieve recognition but because it's the right thing to do. Our focus is to leave a lasting and sustainable legacy for the next generation."

He added, "Landco delivers the physical and institutional infrastructure of the BeachTowns of CaSoBeand Club Laiya to preserve the seaside communities and the localities' unique facets as tourist and leisure destinations. Our team works closely with our property architects, designers, investors, lot owners and the Batangas community at large to foster green and sustainable undertakings."

Landco BeachTowns are master-planned seaside residential and resort communities driven by a powerful vision to create a beachside living destination for the urban pioneers. The eco-friendly properties are designed with aesthetics that remind residents and visitors on the importance of conserving marine life and working in harmony with the natural landscape of the beachside properties.

CaSoBe features modern art installations of lamp posts that resemble a whale's skeleton or ships' frame; roads that are essentially blue to the core as an ode to the sea, and manholes which are painted with images of sea creatures. Landco Pacific Corp. is a subsidiary of Metro Pacific Investments Corporation. For more information about Landco and Leisure Tourism Estates Club Laiya and CaSoBe, visit landco.ph or fb page @LandcoPacificCorporation.
